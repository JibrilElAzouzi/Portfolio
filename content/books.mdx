# My Favorite Books

Here are some books that inspire me and fuel my creativity:

## 1. *Game Engine Architecture* by Jason Gregory
This book offers a comprehensive understanding of game engine design and architecture. Although I'm not finished with it yet, it's already providing valuable insights into how engines work.

---

## 2. *Roblox Lua in 24 Hours* by Chris G. 
A practical guide to learning Lua for Roblox development. It's concise and very helpful for getting up to speed quickly with game scripting.
